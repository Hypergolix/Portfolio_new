About me

Hi, I’m Leon. I’m 20 years old, Swedish, and now reside in Sweden after having lived in the UK for 10 years. I completed both primary and secondary school there, and then studied front-end + React for 6 months at Sundsgården Folkhögskola. I’m really driven, and I like to learn and build projects outside of school. In my spare time I’ve taught myself 3D modelling, 3D-Printing, Arduino/embedded and electronics, and have written some light C/C++ ASCII graphics games. I have some experience hosting LAMP stack webservers but I’m primarily front-end. Some of these projects can be found on my GitHub.
